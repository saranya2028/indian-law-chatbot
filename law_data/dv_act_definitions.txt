Domestic Violence Act — Key Definitions | Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005 | Source: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/

"Domestic violence" includes:
(a) physical abuse — causing bodily pain or harm,
(b) sexual abuse — any conduct of a sexual nature that abuses, humiliates, or degrades,
(c) verbal and emotional abuse — insults, humiliation, name-calling, threats,
(d) economic abuse — deprivation of financial resources, disposal of assets without consent, restricting access to household necessities.

The Act provides protection orders, residence orders, monetary relief, custody orders, and compensation.

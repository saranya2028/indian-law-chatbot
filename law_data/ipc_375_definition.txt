IPC Section 375 | Indian Penal Code, 1860 | Source: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/

A man is said to commit rape if he has sexual intercourse with a woman under circumstances including:
(a) against her will,
(b) without her consent,
(c) with consent obtained by fear or fraud,
(d) with consent when she is intoxicated,
(e) with consent given under a mistaken identity,
(f) with or without her consent when she is under 18 years of age.

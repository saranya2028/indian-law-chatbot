Consumer Rights | Consumer Protection Act, 2019 | Source: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/

Consumers have the following rights:
1. Right to Safety — protection against hazardous goods/services.
2. Right to Information — correct details about product quality, quantity, price, etc.
3. Right to Choose — access to a variety of goods/services at competitive prices.
4. Right to be Heard — consumer grievances must be addressed.
5. Right to Redressal — compensation for defective goods or deficient services.
6. Right to Consumer Education.

Complaints may be filed at District, State, or National Commissions depending on claim value.

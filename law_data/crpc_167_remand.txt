CrPC Section 167 | Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 | Source: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/

When investigation cannot be completed within 24 hours, the accused may be forwarded to a Magistrate who may authorize detention for:
• up to 15 days in police custody,
• up to 90 days for serious offences,
• up to 60 days for other offences.

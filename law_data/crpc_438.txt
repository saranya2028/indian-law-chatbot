CrPC Section 438 | Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 | Source: https://www.indiacode.nic.in/

When any person has reason to believe that they may be arrested on accusation of a non-bailable offence, they may apply to the High Court or Court of Session for anticipatory bail.

The Court may grant interim protection and impose conditions such as:
(a) the person shall make themselves available for interrogation,
(b) shall not make threats or inducements to any person,
(c) shall not leave India without court permission.
